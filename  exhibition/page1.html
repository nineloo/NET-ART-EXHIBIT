Identities

Curated by Niah Llewellyn

A note from the the curator:

All five individual artworks that were carefully selected are from the mind of a black artist creating solely digital or physical artworks that can be pleasantly viewed through a screen. These artworks are meant to be the catalysts to questions around the lines of why so many net artists of color are left out of the conversation. They aren’t meant to be the answers.

While writing this I thought about a quote from Shawné Michaeleain Holloway:

“Now I am having mixed feelings about net art. Because I see how the strategies of black culture, taken and developed by white net artists and institutions are now used by corporations and in politics.”
